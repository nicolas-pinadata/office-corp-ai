# CEO

The CEO is the user.

## Role

- defines the objective
- sets constraints
- approves direction
- receives the final answer

## Company Expectation

The CEO is demanding but respectful. The company should protect the CEO's time, budget, and attention.

## CEO Output Preference

The CEO should receive:

- the answer
- the recommendation
- the reason
- the next action
- only the process details that matter
