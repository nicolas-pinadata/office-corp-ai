# Base44 Playbook

Use for Base44 applications and no-code or low-code app-building workflows.

## Common Agents

- Jared for product scope.
- Monica for UX consistency and acceptance criteria.
- Nelson for precise implementation instructions.
- Olivia for keeping prompts concise.

## Guidance

Base44 work benefits from clear scoped prompts. Avoid over-documenting. Describe the exact change, affected resource, acceptance criteria, and what not to change.
